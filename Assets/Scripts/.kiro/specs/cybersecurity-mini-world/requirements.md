# Cybersecurity Mini-World Requirements

## Introduction

This feature enhances the existing cybersecurity career experience to create a comprehensive mini-world that showcases what cybersecurity professionals do through immersive scenarios, skill-based challenges, and interactive tasks. The goal is to transform the current quest-dialogue system into a full career simulation that meets the game rubric requirements.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Immersive Cybersecurity Environment

**User Story:** As a player exploring careers, I want to experience a realistic cybersecurity workplace environment, so that I can understand what the job setting looks like.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the player enters the cybersecurity mini-world THEN they SHALL see a corporate office environment with security operations center (SOC) elements
2. WHEN the player looks around THEN they SHALL see multiple computer workstations, security monitors, server racks, and network equipment
3. WHEN the player interacts with environmental elements THEN they SHALL receive contextual information about cybersecurity tools and equipment
4. IF the player examines security monitors THEN they SHALL see simulated network traffic, alerts, and security dashboards
5. WHEN the player moves through the space THEN they SHALL encounter different cybersecurity work areas (incident response, threat analysis, network monitoring)

### Requirement 2: Day-in-the-Life Scenario System

**User Story:** As a player, I want to experience realistic cybersecurity scenarios, so that I can understand what cybersecurity professionals do daily.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the player starts the cybersecurity experience THEN they SHALL be presented with a realistic workplace scenario (security breach, threat investigation, etc.)
2. WHEN a scenario begins THEN the system SHALL provide context about the situation, urgency level, and player's role
3. WHEN the player progresses through scenarios THEN they SHALL encounter multiple connected tasks that build on each other
4. IF the player completes a scenario THEN they SHALL see the consequences and impact of their decisions
5. WHEN scenarios are completed THEN the system SHALL provide career insights about what they just experienced

### Requirement 3: Interactive Skill-Based Challenges

**User Story:** As a player, I want to perform hands-on cybersecurity tasks, so that I can develop and test skills relevant to the profession.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the player encounters a security challenge THEN they SHALL interact with simulated cybersecurity tools and interfaces
2. WHEN performing network analysis THEN the player SHALL examine network logs, identify suspicious patterns, and trace attack vectors
3. WHEN investigating incidents THEN the player SHALL collect digital evidence, analyze malware samples, and document findings
4. IF the player attempts password security tasks THEN they SHALL evaluate password strength, implement security policies, and test authentication systems
5. WHEN completing challenges THEN the system SHALL provide real-time feedback on cybersecurity best practices
6. WHEN challenges are time-sensitive THEN the player SHALL work under realistic pressure similar to actual cybersecurity incidents

### Requirement 4: Progressive Career Learning Path

**User Story:** As a player, I want to understand different cybersecurity specializations and career progression, so that I can see potential career paths.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the player completes basic tasks THEN they SHALL unlock more advanced cybersecurity scenarios
2. WHEN the player demonstrates competency THEN they SHALL be introduced to specialized areas (penetration testing, forensics, compliance, etc.)
3. WHEN exploring different areas THEN the player SHALL meet NPCs representing different cybersecurity roles and seniority levels
4. IF the player shows interest in a specialization THEN they SHALL receive information about required skills, education, and career progression
5. WHEN the experience concludes THEN the player SHALL receive a summary of cybersecurity career paths they explored

### Requirement 5: Realistic Tool and Technology Simulation

**User Story:** As a player, I want to use simulated versions of real cybersecurity tools, so that I can understand what technologies professionals use.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the player performs security analysis THEN they SHALL use simplified versions of real cybersecurity tools (SIEM dashboards, network analyzers, etc.)
2. WHEN investigating threats THEN the player SHALL work with realistic data formats (log files, network packets, system alerts)
3. WHEN using security tools THEN the interface SHALL resemble actual cybersecurity software while remaining accessible to beginners
4. IF the player successfully uses a tool THEN they SHALL learn about its real-world applications and importance
5. WHEN tools are introduced THEN the system SHALL explain their purpose and how they fit into cybersecurity workflows

### Requirement 6: Assessment and Skill Validation

**User Story:** As a player, I want to receive feedback on my cybersecurity problem-solving abilities, so that I can understand my aptitude for the field.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the player completes challenges THEN the system SHALL assess their analytical thinking, attention to detail, and decision-making speed
2. WHEN mistakes are made THEN the player SHALL receive constructive feedback explaining correct approaches and industry best practices
3. WHEN the player demonstrates skills THEN they SHALL receive recognition for competencies relevant to cybersecurity careers
4. IF the player struggles with certain aspects THEN the system SHALL provide additional guidance and alternative learning approaches
5. WHEN the experience ends THEN the player SHALL receive a skills assessment highlighting their strengths and areas for growth in cybersecurity
